Clans

Meta DAO is the first guild within which members can create their own guilds (Clans).

A Clan is a self-govering organization inside the Guild, that organizes education of players and receives a part of income. Now it’s really easy to set up your own guild!

The Guild rents out NFT to the Сlan, receiving its part of income. Guild helps with studying and community management.

The Clan Leader pays a fee in MDGG tokens for the clan creating and expansion. The fee is completely burned to assue the value of token (deflationary model).
